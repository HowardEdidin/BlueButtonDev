BlueButton 2.0 enables Medicare Beneficiaries to connect their data to the applications, services and research programs they trust.

As a developer you are building the next generation of applications that will enable our Medicare Beneficiaries to put their data to good use. For their better health, or to help others.

By registering here and passing the validation checks you will be able to create OAuth2.0 keys that you can add to your web site or mobile app.

With these OAuth keys in place you can prompt your users, who are Medicare beneficiaries, to connect to CMS and give your application permissions to download information on their behalf using HL7 FHIR protocols.
